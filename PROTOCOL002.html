Protocol 002: Shin-Safe Mission

Week 2 Training Plan

Goal: Maintain Aerobic Base & Target 29:00 5K PB

ATTENTION: SHIN-SAFE EDITION IN EFFECT

Focus on low impact, controlled movement, and diligent pre/post-run care to prevent injury.

MONDAY:
HYBRID POWER

Phase 1: Mobility & Strength

  • Warm-up: 10--15 min Dynamic
  • Gym Focus: Lower-Body/Knee Stability (RDLs, Step-ups)

Phase 2: Cross-Training

  • Rowing: 20 min Zone 2 (Steady)
  • Bike: 15 min Gentle Spin (90-95 rpm)

TUESDAY:
TEMPO EFFORT

WARNING: Check Shin Status. Rest/Cycle if tender.

Run Sequence: 4.5-5 km

  • Core Segment: 3 km Tempo
  • Target Pace: 6:30-6:45/km
  • Post-Run: Shin Rolling + Calf Stretch

WEDNESDAY:
RECHARGE

Mission: Full Recovery

  • Primary: 20--30 min Mobility or Yoga
  • Optional: Short Walk
  • If Tender: Ice Massage Protocol

THURSDAY:
LONG ENDURANCE

Long Run

  • Distance: 7.5-8 km
  • Pace Zone: 7:45-8:30/km (Easy)
  • Cadence Focus: 165-170 spm
  • Pre-Run: Glute Bridges + Leg Swings

FRIDAY:
STABILITY BUILD

Lower Body & Core

  • Leg Press: 3x10 (Light)
  • Unilateral: Single-Leg RDL
  • Core Focus: Side Planks + Bird Dogs
  • Ankle Work: Resistance Band Strengthening
  • Finish: 10-15 min Incline Walk

SATURDAY:
5K PB ATTEMPT

Race Day Protocol

  • Target Time: 29:00 (5:48/km)
  • Warm-up: Walk, Jog, 3x30s Strides
  • Strategy: Controlled start, strong finish
  • Post-Event: 10 min Walk + Foam Roll

SUNDAY:
SYSTEM OFFLINE

Mandate: Rest / Active Recovery

Focus: Gentle walk, swim, or yoga only. Priority is hydration and total shin recovery.

POST-MISSION DEBRIEF

  • > How did your shins feel after each run? (Critical Metric)
  • > Did your pacing feel controlled on tempo and long runs?
  • > What recovery or mobility habits helped most this week?
  • > How was your energy before and after the 5K PB attempt?
